Poem: Panda’s Day

Panda wakes up with the sun, 🌞

Story: The Busy Squirrel

In the forest, there lived a squirrel named Sammy. 🐿️ He loved collecting nuts and hiding them in little holes. 🌰 One day, he helped his bird friend find food, and learned that sharing brings the biggest joy. 💖
